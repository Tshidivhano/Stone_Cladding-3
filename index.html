About Stone Cladding

Building with Nature's Finest Materials

Proudly South African

Stone Cladding is a proudly South African company based in Mooi River. We specialize in natural stone construction, with a passion for blending timeless design with structural integrity. With over 10 years of hands-on experience in the stonework industry, we've become trusted providers for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects alike.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the built environment using nature's finest materials. We strive to deliver excellent workmanship, promote sustainable construction practices, and help our clients fall in love with their spaces again.

A Note on Sustainability

Our dry-stacking methods reduce cement use and carbon impact. We believe in preserving nature while building with it.

Our Services

Expert stone solutions tailored to your needs, from supply and installation to restoration and renovation.

Fix & Supply

From sourcing quality stone to expertly installing it, we provide a comprehensive fix and supply solution.

  • Delivery to your site
  • Wide selection of stone textures and colors
  • Affordable bulk pricing

Garden Walls

Our stone garden walls bring elegance and structure to any landscape.

  • Custom heights and finishes
  • Natural borders and retaining options
  • Waterproof and erosion-resistant designs

Retaining Walls

Built to last, our retaining walls are created using dry stack techniques, ensuring strength without cement.

  • Engineering support if required
  • Long lifespan and low maintenance
  • Eco-friendly construction

House Cladding

Transform your home's exterior with stone cladding that protects against the elements and adds timeless beauty.

  • Thermal insulation
  • Noise reduction
  • Superior weatherproofing

Drywalls

Eco-friendly and efficient, our drywalls are crafted with precision and do not rely on adhesives or mortar.

Cleaning & Restoration

We clean and restore old stonework, returning it to its natural beauty. Includes chemical-free options and mold removal.

Renovations & Entrances

Stone cladding adds character and strength to gate pillars, boundary walls, and entranceways.
